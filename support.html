Chicken Nugget Collectors Support

Need help with Chicken Nugget Collectors? Contact Atom and Bits at support@atomandbits.com.

Purchase Help

  • Use Restore Purchases on the level-5 unlock screen to restore the Full Game Unlock for the same Apple ID.
  • The Full Game Unlock is a one-time non-consumable in-app purchase processed by Apple.
  • For billing or refund questions, use Apple's purchase support: reportaproblem.apple.com.

Gameplay Help

  • Levels 1-4 are free and replayable.
  • Level 5 and later levels require the one-time Full Game Unlock.
  • Game progress, coins, skins, and streaks are stored on your device.
